Black Beans and Rice

Description

A simple vegetarian dish containing a complete protein of beans and rice. Dish can be modified to add your favorite sides.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans black beans, drained
  • 1 large vidalia onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • juice of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup cilando, chopped

Steps

  1. Sautee the onion and garlic for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the orange juice and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the black beans and mix well.
  4. Add chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper
  5. Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Add cilantro and remove from heat
  7. Serve over white rice with guacamole if desired
